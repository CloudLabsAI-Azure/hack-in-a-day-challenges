## 1. Health & Wellness Benefits

### Medical Insurance
**Coverage Options:**
- **Basic Plan:** Lower premiums, higher deductible ($2,000 individual / $4,000 family)
- **Standard Plan:** Balanced coverage ($1,000 individual / $2,000 family)
- **Premium Plan:** Comprehensive coverage ($500 individual / $1,000 family)

**Company Contribution:**
- Company covers 80% of employee premiums
- 50% contribution for dependents
- HSA contribution: $500/year for Basic Plan enrollees

**Network:** National PPO network with 50,000+ providers
**Prescription Coverage:** Tier 1 ($10), Tier 2 ($30), Tier 3 ($50)

### Dental Insurance
- Two cleaning visits per year (100% covered)
- Basic procedures (80% covered after deductible)
- Major procedures (50% covered after deductible)
- Annual maximum: $2,000 per person
- Orthodontia coverage available ($2,000 lifetime maximum)

### Vision Insurance
- Annual eye exam (100% covered)
- Frames or contact lenses allowance: $200 every 2 years
- Lens upgrades and coatings covered up to plan limits
- Discounts on LASIK procedures

### Mental Health Support
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 8 free counseling sessions per year
- 24/7 crisis hotline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX
- Mental health services covered same as medical (virtual and in-person)
- Stress management and wellness resources

### Health & Wellness Programs
- **Annual Health Screening:** Free biometric screening and health assessment
- **Fitness Reimbursement:** Up to $50/month for gym memberships or fitness classes
- **Wellness Challenges:** Quarterly challenges with prizes and incentives
- **On-site Wellness:** Yoga classes, ergonomic assessments, flu shots
- **Health Coaching:** Free personalized health coaching and support

## 2. Retirement & Financial Benefits

### 401(k) Retirement Plan
- **Company Match:** 100% match up to 6% of salary
- **Vesting Schedule:** Immediate vesting on all contributions
- **Investment Options:** 20+ fund options across risk profiles
- **Roth 401(k) Option:** Available for after-tax contributions
- **Catch-up Contributions:** Additional $7,500 for employees 50+

**2024 Contribution Limits:**
- Employee: $23,000 annual maximum
- Age 50+: $30,500 with catch-up

### Financial Planning Services
- Free consultation with certified financial planners
- Retirement planning workshops (quarterly)
- Investment education webinars
- College savings planning (529 plans)
- Estate planning guidance

### Life Insurance
**Company-Provided:**
- Basic Life Insurance: 1x annual salary (up to $250,000)
- Accidental Death & Dismemberment: 1x annual salary

**Voluntary Coverage:**
- Supplemental Life: Up to 5x salary (employee pays premium)
- Spouse Coverage: Up to $250,000
- Child Coverage: $10,000 per child

### Disability Insurance
**Short-Term Disability (Company-paid):**
- 60% of salary for up to 90 days
- Waiting period: 7 days

**Long-Term Disability (Company-paid):**
- 60% of salary after 90 days
- Coverage until age 65 or return to work

## 6. Enrollment & Eligibility

### New Hire Enrollment
**Timeline:**
- Benefits eligible on first day of month following hire date
- Enrollment window: 30 days from hire date
- Coverage effective: First of month following enrollment

**Required Actions:**
1. Review benefits guide and plan options
2. Attend new hire benefits orientation
3. Complete enrollment through employee portal
4. Designate beneficiaries for life insurance and 401(k)
5. Submit dependent verification documents

### Annual Open Enrollment
- **Period:** November 1-30 each year
- **Changes Effective:** January 1 of following year
- **Required:** Active enrollment decision each year
- **Communication:** Email reminders and information sessions

### Qualifying Life Events
You can make mid-year benefit changes within 31 days of:
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption of child
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status
- Death of dependent

### Eligibility

**Full-Time Employees (30+ hours/week):**
- All health and welfare benefits
- 401(k) with company match
- Full PTO accrual
- All voluntary benefits

**Part-Time Employees (20-29 hours/week):**
- Medical, dental, vision options available
- 401(k) with company match
- Prorated PTO accrual
- Life and disability insurance

**Dependents Eligible for Coverage:**
- Legal spouse or domestic partner
- Children up to age 26
- Disabled dependents (no age limit)

### Cost & Payroll Deductions

**Medical Insurance (bi-weekly costs):**
- Employee Only: $45-$120 depending on plan
- Employee + Spouse: $110-$290
- Employee + Child(ren): $95-$250
- Family: $150-$380

**Other Benefits (bi-weekly):**
- Dental: $8 (employee), $22 (family)
- Vision: $3 (employee), $7 (family)
- Supplemental Life: Varies by age and coverage amount
