# Employee Onboarding Guide

**Welcome to the Team!**

## Week 1: Getting Started

### Day 1 - Welcome & Orientation
**8:30 AM - HR Welcome Session**
- Complete new hire paperwork (Tax forms, banking details, emergency contacts)
- Receive employee ID badge and access cards
- Review employee handbook and company policies
- Sign confidentiality and acceptable use agreements

**10:00 AM - IT Setup**
- Collect laptop, monitor, and accessories
- Set up email account and passwords
- Install required software applications
- Configure VPN and security tools
- Access employee portal and self-service systems

**1:00 PM - Manager Meeting**
- Introduction to team members
- Overview of role responsibilities
- Review 30-60-90 day goals
- Schedule regular 1-on-1 meetings

**3:00 PM - Workspace Setup**
- Desk assignment and tour of facilities
- Office supplies and equipment
- Parking pass or transit information
- Building security and emergency procedures

### Day 2 - Company Overview
- Company history, mission, and values presentation
- Organizational structure and key stakeholders
- Product/service portfolio overview
- Customer base and market position
- Introduction to company culture and expectations

### Day 3 - Department Deep Dive
- Meet with department leadership
- Overview of department goals and projects
- Introduction to team workflows and processes
- Review communication channels (Slack, Teams, email protocols)
- Access to shared drives and documentation

### Day 4 - Role-Specific Training
- Job-specific systems and tools training
- Review of current projects and priorities
- Shadow team members on key tasks
- Begin assigned training modules in learning platform

### Day 5 - Week 1 Check-In
- HR check-in meeting to address questions
- Benefits enrollment discussion
- Review of first week experiences
- Set up mentor/buddy program pairing

## Week 2-4: Building Knowledge

### Training & Development
- Complete mandatory compliance training (Safety, Ethics, Data Privacy)
- Department-specific technical training
- Customer interaction protocols (if applicable)
- Quality standards and procedures
- Time tracking and expense reporting systems

### Relationship Building
- Schedule introductory meetings with key stakeholders
- Attend team meetings and observe decision-making processes
- Participate in company social events
- Join relevant employee resource groups or committees

### Performance Goals
- Finalize 30-60-90 day performance objectives
- Understand performance review process and timeline
- Identify learning and development opportunities
- Set up regular feedback sessions with manager

## First 90 Days: Key Milestones

### 30-Day Goals
- Understand company products, services, and value proposition
- Build relationships with immediate team members
- Complete all onboarding training requirements
- Demonstrate basic competency in core job functions
- Identify areas for additional support or training

### 60-Day Goals
- Take on project responsibilities with minimal supervision
- Contribute ideas in team meetings
- Demonstrate understanding of departmental workflows
- Build relationships with cross-functional partners
- Complete first performance check-in with manager

### 90-Day Goals
- Perform job responsibilities independently
- Meet established performance targets
- Actively contribute to team goals and projects
- Identify opportunities for process improvements
- Complete 90-day performance review

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Payroll runs bi-weekly on Fridays. Your first paycheck will be on the second Friday after your start date.

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Submit leave requests through the employee portal at least 2 weeks in advance for planned time off.

**Q: What is the dress code?**
A: Business casual. Jeans are acceptable on Fridays. Client-facing roles may require business professional attire.

**Q: Can I work remotely?**
A: Remote work policies vary by department. Discuss options with your manager after the first 90 days.

**Q: Who do I contact for IT issues?**
A: IT Help Desk at (555) 123-4000 or submit a ticket at helpdesk.company.com.

**Q: When am I eligible for benefits?**
A: Benefits begin on the first day of the month following your start date. Enrollment window is within 30 days of hire.
