# Invoice & Payment Terms Policy

**Accounts Payable Guidelines**  
**Version 2.5 | Effective January 2024**

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- **Net 30:** Payment due 30 days from invoice date (default for most vendors)
- **Net 60:** Extended terms for approved strategic vendors
- **Net 15:** Expedited payment for small vendors or urgent needs
- **Due Upon Receipt:** Immediate payment required (utilities, subscriptions)

### Early Payment Discounts
- **2/10 Net 30:** 2% discount if paid within 10 days, otherwise due in 30 days
- **1/15 Net 30:** 1% discount if paid within 15 days
- Always take discounts when cash flow allows - effective annual interest rate is significant

## Invoice Submission Requirements

### Required Information
All invoices must include:
- Vendor legal name and address
- Vendor tax ID or business registration number
- Invoice number (unique identifier)
- Invoice date
- Purchase order number (if PO issued)
- Detailed description of goods/services
- Quantity, unit price, and total amount
- Payment terms and due date
- Remittance instructions (bank details or mailing address)

### Preferred Submission Methods
1. **Email:** ap@company.com (PDF format preferred)
2. **Vendor Portal:** suppliers.company.com
3. **Mail:** Accounts Payable, PO Box 12345, City, State 12345
4. **Electronic Invoice:** EDI or API integration for high-volume vendors

### Invoice Format
- PDF format preferred for email submissions
- Itemized detail required (not just lump sum)
- Original invoice only (no duplicates)
- One invoice per email for easier processing
- Separate invoices by cost center or project

## Approval Process

### Three-Way Match
All invoices require matching of:
1. **Purchase Order** - Authorized PO in system
2. **Invoice** - Vendor bill with correct details
3. **Receipt** - Goods received or services performed confirmation

### Approval Workflow
**Step 1: Invoice Receipt (Day 1)**
- AP logs invoice in system
- Assigns to appropriate approver
- Matches to PO (if applicable)

**Step 2: Manager Review (Day 1-3)**
- Department manager verifies accuracy
- Confirms goods/services received
- Approves or rejects with comments
- Escalates discrepancies

**Step 3: Finance Review (Day 4-5)**
- AP verifies approval chain
- Checks for duplicate invoices
- Validates account coding
- Flags any policy violations

**Step 4: Payment Processing (Day 6-28)**
- Approved invoices scheduled for payment
- Batched by payment date and method
- Released according to payment terms
- Payment confirmation sent to vendor

### Approval Authority Limits
- **Department Manager:** Up to $5,000
- **Director:** Up to $25,000
- **VP:** Up to $100,000
- **CFO:** Up to $500,000
- **CEO:** Above $500,000

### Rush Payment Requests
- Submit to ap-urgent@company.com with justification
- Requires VP approval
- Additional $50 processing fee may apply
- Must be truly urgent (penalties, service cutoff, etc.)

## Payment Methods

### ACH/Wire Transfer (Preferred)
- Domestic: ACH (no fee)
- International: Wire transfer ($25-45 fee)
- Requires completed vendor W-9 and banking information
- Most secure and efficient method
- Payment typically arrives in 2-3 business days (ACH) or same day (wire)

### Check
- Standard check run: Weekly on Thursdays
- Check mailed via USPS (5-7 business days to receive)
- Overnight delivery available for urgent situations ($25 fee)
- Being phased out; encourage vendors to switch to ACH

### Credit Card
- Available for subscriptions and recurring charges
- One-time purchases up to $5,000
- Vendor must accept credit card (3% processing fee)
- Corporate card program for authorized users

### Virtual Card
- Single-use card numbers for specific transactions
- Enhanced security and rebate opportunities
- Preferred for large one-time purchases
- Contact AP for virtual card setup

## Common Scenarios

### Recurring Invoices
**Monthly Subscriptions:**
- Software licenses, utilities, maintenance
- Auto-approved if amount within 5% of prior month
- Annual review to confirm continued need
- Set up as recurring payment when possible

**Example:** Office rent, SaaS subscriptions, cleaning services

### Milestone-Based Payments
**Project Work:**
- Payment tied to deliverable completion
- Requires project manager sign-off
- Partial payments for multi-milestone projects
- Final payment upon project acceptance

**Example:** Construction, consulting projects, software development

### Retainer Agreements
**Professional Services:**
- Monthly retainer billed in advance
- Additional work beyond retainer requires separate invoice
- Annual reconciliation of actual vs. retainer
- Contract must specify retainer terms

**Example:** Legal, accounting, PR firms

### Reimbursement to Employees
**Expense Reports:**
- Submit through expense system (separate from AP process)
- Requires receipts and manager approval
- Reimbursed via payroll direct deposit
- Due within 30 days of expense date

See Travel & Expense Reimbursement Policy for details.

## Disputed Invoices

### Common Disputes
- Goods not received or damaged
- Services not performed as contracted
- Pricing different from agreement
- Incorrect quantities or calculations
- Missing required documentation
- Duplicate invoice

### Resolution Process
1. **Hold Payment:** Invoice placed on hold pending resolution
2. **Vendor Notification:** AP contacts vendor with dispute details
3. **Investigation:** Department manager works with vendor to resolve
4. **Resolution Options:**
   - Credit memo issued by vendor
   - Revised invoice submitted
   - Partial payment for acceptable portion
   - Full payment if dispute unfounded
5. **Payment Release:** Once resolved, payment processed in next cycle

**Timeline:** Most disputes resolved within 14 days

### Escalation
- Unresolved disputes >30 days escalate to procurement
- Legal involvement for contract disputes
- May hold additional invoices until resolution

## Compliance & Controls

### Tax Compliance
**1099 Reporting:**
- Annual 1099s issued to US vendors for services >$600
- Requires completed W-9 on file
- Vendors must provide correct tax ID
- Penalty for incorrect 1099s: Up to $280 per form

**Sales Tax:**
- Sales tax collected on applicable purchases
- Exemption certificates maintained for exempt purchases
- Compliance with multi-state tax regulations

**International Withholding:**
- 30% withholding required on payments to foreign vendors without W-8
- Reduced rates available under tax treaties
- Vendor responsible for providing proper documentation

### Audit & Controls
**Internal Controls:**
- Segregation of duties (approval vs. payment)
- Duplicate invoice detection
- Vendor master file review
- Payment authorization limits
- Regular vendor statement reconciliation

**Audit Trail:**
- Full invoice approval history maintained
- Payment batch reports archived
- Vendor communications documented
- Exception reports reviewed monthly

### Fraud Prevention
**Red Flags:**
- Requests to change banking information
- Pressure for immediate payment
- Invoices from new vendors without PO
- Unusual payment methods requested
- Vendor contact information doesn't match records

**Prevention Measures:**
- Verify bank changes via phone to known vendor contact
- Multi-person approval for new vendors
- Regular vendor master file review
- Employee training on fraud schemes
- Report suspicious activity to ap-fraud@company.com

## Payment Schedule

### Standard Payment Cycle
**Weekly Check Run:**
- Processed: Every Thursday
- Mailed: Friday (arrives following week)

**ACH Payment:**
- Processed: Twice weekly (Tuesday & Thursday)
- Funds available: 2-3 business days

**Wire Transfer:**
- Processed: Daily (by 2 PM cutoff)
- Funds available: Same day (domestic) or 1-2 days (international)

### Holiday Schedule
- No processing on company holidays
- Payment dates moved to prior business day
- Holiday schedule published in December for following year

### Month-End/Year-End
- Accelerated processing for month-end accruals
- Year-end cutoff: December 27th for December invoices
- January invoices held until new fiscal year

## Vendor FAQs

**Q: How long until my invoice is paid?**
A: Standard terms are Net 30 from invoice date. If approved within 5 days of receipt, payment typically sent 2-3 days before due date.

**Q: Why hasn't my invoice been paid?**
A: Check status at suppliers.company.com or email ap-status@company.com with invoice number. Common reasons: awaiting approval, missing information, on hold for dispute.

**Q: How do I sign up for ACH payment?**
A: Complete ACH authorization form at suppliers.company.com/ach. Effective for invoices approved after form processed (5-7 days).

**Q: Can you expedite payment?**
A: Rush requests considered for genuine emergencies (service cutoff, penalty avoidance). Email ap-urgent@company.com with justification. Additional fee may apply.

**Q: Who do I contact about a missing payment?**
A: Email ap-status@company.com with invoice number and date. We'll research payment status and reissue if necessary (allow 7-10 days for replacement check).

**Q: How do I update my company information?**
A: Log in to supplier portal at suppliers.company.com to update address, contact, banking info. For tax ID or legal name changes, submit new W-9.

**Q: Why do you need a W-9 from me?**
A: IRS requires W-9 for all US vendors for 1099 tax reporting. Cannot process payments without valid W-9 on file.

**Q: Do you accept credit card payments?**
A: We pay by ACH or check. We don't accept credit cards for invoice payment. If you're asking about paying your invoice by credit card, contact your AR team.
