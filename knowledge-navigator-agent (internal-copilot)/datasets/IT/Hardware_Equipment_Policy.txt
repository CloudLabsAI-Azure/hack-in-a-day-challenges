# Hardware & Equipment Request Policy

**IT Department | Version 2.3**

## Purpose
Establish guidelines for requesting, approving, and managing company-issued hardware and equipment.

## Standard Equipment Packages

### New Hire Package
**Provided on Day 1:**
- Laptop (Windows or Mac based on role)
- Monitor (24" or 27")
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset with microphone
- Webcam (if laptop camera insufficient)
- Laptop bag
- Power adapter and cables
- Docking station (if applicable)

**Automatic Based on Role:**
- Developers: 32GB RAM, upgraded processor
- Designers: Mac with 64GB RAM, large monitor
- Sales: Mobile hotspot device
- Executives: Premium laptop, dual monitors

### Mobile Devices
**Eligibility:**
- Managers and above (automatic)
- Sales and field staff (automatic)
- On-call IT/support staff (automatic)
- Others by business need (manager approval)

**Options:**
- iPhone 15 or Samsung Galaxy S24 (standard)
- iPad or Surface tablet (role-dependent)
- Company pays plan + device
- BYOD option available (limited support)

## Equipment Refresh Cycle

### Automatic Replacement
- **Laptops:** Every 3 years
- **Desktops:** Every 4 years
- **Monitors:** Every 5 years
- **Phones:** Every 2 years
- **Tablets:** Every 3 years

**Process:**
- IT sends notification 60 days before refresh date
- Schedule appointment for device swap
- Data backed up and transferred
- Old device wiped and recycled
- No cost to employee or department

### Early Replacement
**Reasons for Early Replacement:**
- Device malfunction beyond repair
- Performance inadequate for job requirements
- Role change requiring different specs
- Physical damage (accidental damage fee may apply)
- Lost or stolen device

**Approval Required:**
- Manager justification
- IT assessment
- Budget availability
- VP approval if >$2,000

## Additional Equipment Requests

### Monitors
**Standard Allowance:**
- One 24" monitor included with laptop
- Developers/designers: Two monitors standard
- Request additional monitor via IT portal

**Upgrade Options:**
- 27" monitor: Manager approval
- 32" or ultrawide: Director approval
- 4K monitor: Role justification required
- Dual monitor arm: Included if space permits

### Peripherals
**Available Equipment:**
- Ergonomic keyboard ($50-150)
- Vertical mouse or trackball
- Laptop stand
- USB hub or dock
- External hard drive (encrypted)
- Webcam upgrade
- Ring light for video calls
- Document camera

**Approval:**
- Standard items <$100: IT approval
- Ergonomic needs: Manager + IT approval
- Specialized equipment >$200: Director approval

### Home Office Setup
**Remote Workers:**
- One-time $500 stipend for home office setup
- Desk and chair reimbursement (submit receipts)
- Additional monitor provided
- Upgraded webcam/headset if needed
- Ergonomic assessment available

**What's Covered:**
- Desk (up to $300)
- Office chair (up to $250)
- Desk lamp/lighting
- Keyboard/mouse tray
- Monitor arm
- Cable management

### Specialized Equipment
**By Role/Need:**
- Drawing tablets (designers)
- High-end graphics cards (3D work)
- Additional RAM or SSD upgrades
- Portable monitors for travel
- Presentation remotes
- External microphones
- Studio lighting

**Requires:**
- Detailed business justification
- Manager and director approval
- Budget confirmation
- May require VP approval if >$1,000

## Request Process

### Standard Requests
1. Log in to IT Service Portal: helpdesk.company.com
2. Click "Hardware Request"
3. Select equipment type and specifications
4. Provide business justification
5. Confirm budget/cost center
6. Submit request
7. Manager auto-notified for approval
8. IT processes and orders
9. Notification when ready for pickup

**Timeline:**
- In-stock items: 1-3 business days
- Custom builds: 1-2 weeks
- Special orders: 2-4 weeks

### Emergency Requests
**Device Failure:**
- Call IT Help Desk: (555) 123-4000
- Loaner device provided same day
- Permanent replacement ordered
- Data restored from backup

**Lost/Stolen:**
- Report immediately to IT Security: (555) 123-4100
- File police report for stolen devices
- Remote wipe initiated
- Replacement approved if:
  - First occurrence: No cost
  - Second occurrence: Manager approval + cost review
  - Third+ occurrence: Employee may pay deductible ($250-500)

## Equipment Management

### Asset Tracking
**All Equipment Tagged:**
- Asset tag sticker applied
- Recorded in IT asset management system
- Assigned to specific employee
- Tracked through lifecycle

**Employee Responsibilities:**
- Note asset tag number
- Report changes (location, user)
- Don't remove or obscure asset tags
- Annual asset verification survey

### Maintenance & Repairs

**Covered Under Warranty:**
- Manufacturing defects
- Normal wear and tear
- Hardware failures
- Battery replacement (if defective)

**Not Covered:**
- Accidental damage (drops, spills, cracks)
- Cosmetic damage not affecting function
- Lost or stolen devices
- Unauthorized modifications
- Damage from misuse

**Repair Process:**
1. Submit IT ticket describing issue
2. IT diagnoses problem
3. If repairable: Send to repair center (5-10 days)
4. Loaner device provided during repair
5. If not repairable: Replacement authorized

### Upgrades
**Allowed Upgrades:**
- RAM (if slots available)
- Storage (SSD upgrade or addition)
- Battery replacement
- Screen protector or privacy filter

**Not Allowed:**
- Opening device yourself
- Installing non-approved hardware
- Upgrading beyond manufacturer specs
- Modifications voiding warranty

**Request Upgrade:**
- Submit via IT portal
- Justify business need
- IT schedules upgrade appointment
- IT performs upgrade (don't DIY)

## Security & Compliance

### Device Security
**Required:**
- Full disk encryption enabled
- Antivirus/endpoint protection installed
- Screen lock after 5 minutes
- Strong password/PIN
- Biometric login (fingerprint/face) when available
- Auto-update enabled
- VPN for remote access

**Prohibited:**
- Disabling security software
- Sharing device with others
- Installing unauthorized software
- Jailbreaking/rooting mobile devices
- Using admin/root accounts for daily work

### Data Protection
**Best Practices:**
- Save files to OneDrive/SharePoint (auto-backed up)
- Don't store sensitive data locally
- Encrypt removable drives
- Use password manager
- Enable Find My Device

**Before Repair/Return:**
- Back up important files
- Sign out of all accounts
- Remove personal data
- IT will perform full wipe

### Lost or Stolen Devices
**Immediate Actions:**
1. Call IT Security: (555) 123-4100 (24/7)
2. File police report if stolen
3. Report to manager
4. Change all passwords

**IT Actions:**
- Remote lock device
- Attempt to locate (if tracking enabled)
- Remote wipe if necessary
- Disable access to corporate systems
- Investigate incident

**Replacement:**
- Replacement approved after investigation
- New device provisioned
- Security review if data may be compromised

## Return & Disposal

### When to Return Equipment
**Required Return:**
- Employment termination (last day)
- Extended leave (>90 days)
- Role change (if equipment no longer needed)
- Equipment refresh (swap old for new)
- No longer needed

**Return Process:**
1. Contact IT to schedule return
2. Back up personal files
3. Sign out of all accounts
4. Return all equipment and accessories
5. IT wipes device
6. Receipt provided

**What to Return:**
- Laptop/desktop
- Monitors
- Keyboard, mouse, accessories
- Mobile devices
- Docking station
- Power adapters and cables
- Laptop bag
- Any other IT equipment

### Equipment Disposal
**IT Handles:**
- Secure data wiping (DoD standards)
- Hard drive destruction (if needed)
- Environmentally responsible recycling
- Certificate of destruction provided
- Compliance with data protection regulations

**Never:**
- Throw equipment in trash
- Donate company equipment without authorization
- Take home old equipment
- Sell or give away company devices

## BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

### BYOD Policy
**Limited Support:**
- Email and calendar on personal phone
- Microsoft Teams
- Web-based applications
- VPN access

**Requirements:**
- Enroll in Mobile Device Management (MDM)
- Company can wipe corporate data remotely
- Meet minimum security standards
- Keep OS and apps updated
- Enable encryption and passcode

**Not Supported:**
- Personal laptops for primary work
- Installing company software on personal devices
- IT troubleshooting of personal device issues
- Repairs or replacements
- Confidential data on personal devices

**Stipend:**
- $50/month mobile device stipend (if approved)
- Reimburse portion of phone bill
- Submit monthly receipt

## FAQs

**Q: Can I choose Mac or Windows?**
A: Based on role. Developers and designers often get choice. Business users typically Windows. Discuss with manager.

**Q: Can I upgrade my laptop RAM?**
A: Request through IT. We'll assess if upgrade is possible and appropriate. Don't open device yourself.

**Q: What if I spill coffee on my laptop?**
A: Report to IT immediately. May be repairable. First incident usually covered; repeated accidents may incur fees.

**Q: Can I take equipment home?**
A: Yes, laptops designed for mobile use. Desktops/monitors stay at office unless remote worker.

**Q: What happens to my old equipment?**
A: Securely wiped and recycled or repurposed for other users if still functional.

**Q: Can I buy my old equipment when I get a refresh?**
A: Generally no. Equipment is recycled. Exceptions rare and require approval.

**Q: Who do I contact for equipment issues?**
A: IT Help Desk at (555) 123-4000 or helpdesk@company.com.
