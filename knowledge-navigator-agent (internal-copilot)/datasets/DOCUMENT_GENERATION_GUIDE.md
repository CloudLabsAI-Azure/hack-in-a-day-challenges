# Sample Document Templates for Knowledge Navigator

This folder contains templates and scripts to help you quickly generate the 12 required department documents for your Internal Knowledge Navigator agent.

## Quick Start - Generate All Documents

You can use AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, or Microsoft Copilot) to generate realistic policy documents. Here are ready-to-use prompts:

## HR Department Documents

### 1. HR Leave Policy

**AI Generation Prompt:**
```
Create a comprehensive 2,500-word HR Leave Policy document for a mid-sized tech company called "Contoso Corporation" that includes:

STRUCTURE:
- Company Overview and Policy Purpose
- Leave Entitlements
  - Annual Leave: 15 days per year (accrued monthly at 1.25 days/month)
  - Sick Leave: 10 days per year with doctor's note required after 3 consecutive days
  - Personal Leave: 3 days per year for family emergencies
  - Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family
  - Parental Leave: 12 weeks paid
- Leave Request Process
  - Submit request via HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance
  - Manager approval required within 3 business days
  - HR confirmation sent within 24 hours
- Carryover Policy
  - Maximum 5 days can be carried to next year
  - Must be used within Q1 of following year
- Public Holidays
  - 10 recognized public holidays
  - Pro-rated for part-time employees
- Unpaid Leave
  - Available after exhausting paid leave
  - Requires department head approval
- Leave Balance Tracking
  - Check balance in employee portal
  - Monthly statements sent automatically

Use professional HR language, include specific procedures, approval workflows, and contact information (HR Dept: hr@contoso.com, Phone: ext. 5100).

Format with clear headings, bullet points, numbered procedures, and a table showing leave types with entitlements.
```

### 2. HR Onboarding Guide

**AI Generation Prompt:**
```
Create a detailed 2,000-word Employee Onboarding Guide for Contoso Corporation including:

SECTIONS:
1. Welcome to Contoso
   - Company mission and values
   - First day expectations

2. Pre-Day One Checklist (sent 1 week before)
   - Confirm start date and time (9:00 AM)
   - Required documents: ID, bank details, emergency contact
   - Parking or transit information

3. First Day Schedule
   - 9:00 AM: Arrive at reception, get visitor badge
   - 9:15 AM: Meet HR representative
   - 9:30 AM: IT equipment setup (laptop, phone, access cards)
   - 10:30 AM: Office tour and introductions
   - 11:30 AM: Lunch with team
   - 1:00 PM: Benefits enrollment session
   - 2:30 PM: Department meeting with manager
   - 4:00 PM: Day 1 wrap-up with buddy

4. First Week Activities
   - Day 2: Training sessions and system access
   - Day 3: Meet department stakeholders
   - Day 4: Shadow experienced team member
   - Day 5: First assignment and end-of-week check-in

5. Required Paperwork
   - Tax forms (W-4 or equivalent)
   - Direct deposit authorization
   - Emergency contact form
   - Confidentiality agreement
   - Employee handbook acknowledgment

6. IT Setup
   - Email account activation
   - VPN configuration
   - Required software installations
   - Security training completion

7. Benefits Enrollment
   - Health insurance (enrollment deadline: 30 days)
   - Retirement plan (401k)
   - Life insurance
   - Flexible spending accounts

8. 30-60-90 Day Milestones
   - Week 1: Complete onboarding paperwork and training
   - Month 1: Understand role responsibilities
   - Month 2: Begin contributing to projects
   - Month 3: Performance check-in with manager

Include contact information for HR (hr@contoso.com), IT Helpdesk (it-support@contoso.com, ext. 5200), and buddy program coordinator.

Use friendly, welcoming tone with clear checklists and timelines.
```

### 3. HR Benefits Guide

**AI Generation Prompt:**
```
Create a comprehensive 3,500-word Employee Benefits Guide for Contoso Corporation covering:

SECTIONS:
1. Benefits Overview
   - Total compensation philosophy
   - Benefits eligibility (full-time employees after 30 days)

2. Health Insurance
   - Medical Plans:
     * PPO Plan: $150/month employee contribution, $300 family
     * HMO Plan: $100/month employee contribution, $200 family
     * Deductibles, co-pays, out-of-pocket maximums
   - Dental Insurance: $20/month, includes preventive, basic, major care
   - Vision Insurance: $10/month, annual eye exam, glasses/contacts allowance
   - Coverage effective: 1st of month following hire date

3. Retirement Plans
   - 401(k) Retirement Plan
     * Employee contribution: up to IRS limit
     * Company match: 50% of first 6% contributed
     * Vesting schedule: 20% per year, fully vested after 5 years
     * Investment options: 15 fund choices
   - Pension Plan (for employees hired before 2020)
     * Based on years of service and final average salary

4. Life and Disability Insurance
   - Basic Life Insurance: 1x annual salary (company-paid)
   - Supplemental Life: Available for purchase up to 5x salary
   - Short-term Disability: 60% salary replacement after 7-day wait
   - Long-term Disability: 60% salary replacement after 90 days

5. Flexible Spending Accounts
   - Healthcare FSA: Up to $3,050/year (tax-free)
   - Dependent Care FSA: Up to $5,000/year
   - Use-it-or-lose-it rule applies (except $610 carryover for healthcare)

6. Paid Time Off (Summary)
   - Annual Leave: 15 days
   - Sick Leave: 10 days
   - Personal Days: 3 days
   - See full Leave Policy for details

7. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
   - Confidential counseling services
   - 6 free sessions per year per family member
   - Financial planning assistance
   - Legal consultation
   - Available 24/7: 1-800-EAP-HELP

8. Wellness Programs
   - On-site fitness center (free membership)
   - Annual health screening
   - Flu shot clinics
   - Wellness challenges with prizes
   - Ergonomic assessments

9. Tuition Reimbursement
   - Up to $5,000/year for approved degree programs
   - 75% reimbursement with C grade or higher
   - Professional certifications covered 100%
   - Requires 1-year service commitment after completion

10. Commuter Benefits
    - Pre-tax transit pass program
    - Parking reimbursement: up to $100/month
    - Bike-to-work incentive: $50/month

11. Employee Discounts
    - Corporate partnerships: electronics, travel, entertainment
    - Discount portal access

12. Enrollment and Changes
    - Annual open enrollment: November 1-30
    - Qualifying life events allow mid-year changes
    - Contact HR within 30 days of life event

Include benefits comparison tables, enrollment instructions, contact information (Benefits Team: benefits@contoso.com, ext. 5110), and links to benefit provider websites.

Use clear, jargon-free language with specific dollar amounts and percentages.
```

## Finance Department Documents

### 4. Finance Expense Policy

**AI Generation Prompt:**
```
Create a detailed 2,800-word Business Expense Policy for Contoso Corporation including:

SECTIONS:
1. Policy Overview
   - Purpose: Ensure appropriate business expense management
   - Scope: All employees incurring business expenses

2. Expense Approval Authority
   - Up to $500: Manager approval
   - $500 - $2,500: Department head approval  
   - $2,500 - $10,000: VP approval + Finance review
   - Over $10,000: CFO approval required

3. Eligible Business Expenses
   - Client meals and entertainment
   - Business travel (see Travel Policy)
   - Office supplies and equipment
   - Professional development and training
   - Client gifts (max $100 per gift)
   - Subscription services related to work
   - Professional association memberships

4. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
   - Personal entertainment
   - Alcoholic beverages (except client dinners)
   - Traffic violations and parking tickets
   - Personal grooming
   - Commuting costs (home to office)
   - Spouse/family travel (unless approved)

5. Meal and Entertainment Guidelines
   - Business meals: Actual cost with receipts
   - Client entertainment: Pre-approval required over $200
   - Team meals: Max $30 per person
   - Conference meals: Use per diem rates if not provided
   - Gratuity: 15-20% is acceptable

6. Receipt Requirements
   - Original itemized receipts required for all expenses over $25
   - Missing receipts: Require signed Missing Receipt Affidavit
   - Credit card statements alone are not sufficient
   - Receipts must show:
     * Vendor name and date
     * Itemized list of purchases
     * Payment method
     * Names of attendees for meals

7. Corporate Credit Cards
   - Eligibility: Managers and above, frequent travelers
   - Card limits based on role
   - Personal charges prohibited
   - Statement reconciliation due 5 business days after close
   - Lost/stolen cards: Report immediately to Finance

8. Expense Report Submission
   - Submit within 30 days of expense date
   - Use online expense system: expenses.contoso.com
   - Include business purpose for each expense
   - Attach all receipts (scan or photo)
   - Manager approval required before submission

9. Reimbursement Timeline
   - Processing time: 5-7 business days after approval
   - Direct deposit to employee bank account
   - Pay period: Bi-weekly
   - Check reimbursement requests: financehelp@contoso.com

10. Mileage Reimbursement
    - Personal vehicle for business: $0.655 per mile (current IRS rate)
    - Must log: Date, purpose, start/end locations, miles
    - Not applicable for commuting

11. Exceptions and Escalations
    - Exceptions require CFO approval
    - Extraordinary expenses: Document justification
    - Contact Finance for guidance: ext. 5300

12. Policy Violations
    - First violation: Written warning
    - Repeated violations: Suspension of privileges
    - Fraudulent claims: Disciplinary action up to termination

Include expense category examples, approval workflow diagram, and frequently asked questions.

Contact: Finance Department - financehelp@contoso.com, ext. 5300
```
