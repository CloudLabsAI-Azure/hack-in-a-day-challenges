# Getting started with Challenge Environment
**Overall Estimated Duration : 4 Hours**  
Welcome to the lab. We’ve prepared a seamless environment for you to explore and learn. Let’s begin by making the most of this experience.

## Accessing Your Challenge Environment
Once you’re ready to dive in, your virtual machine and lab guide will be right at your fingertips within your web browser.  
![](images/vmandguidev2.png)

## Exploring Your Challenge Resources
To get a better understanding of your lab resources and credentials, navigate to the **Environment Details** tab.  
![](images/env-details.png)

## Utilizing the Split Window Feature
For convenience, you can open the lab guide in a separate window by selecting the **Split Window** button from the top right corner.  
![](images/spl.png)

## Managing Your Virtual Machine
Feel free to start, stop, or restart your virtual machine as needed from the **Resources** tab. Your experience is in your hands.  
![](images/resources.png)

## Let’s Get Started with Azure Portal
On your virtual machine, click on the Azure Portal icon and sign in to the **Azure Portal** (<http://portal.azure.com>).  
On the **Sign in to Microsoft Azure** page, enter your credentials and select **Next**.

**Azure Username/Email:** `<inject key="AzureAdUserEmail"></inject>`  
**Azure Password:** `<inject key="AzureAdUserPassword"></inject>`

**Note:** Refer to the **Environment Details** tab for any other lab credentials or details.  
![](images/image-004.jpg)  
![](images/image-005.jpg)

If you see a pop-up that says **Skip for now (14 days until this is required)**, select it.  
![](images/image004.png)

If prompted **Stay Signed in?**, click **No**.  
![](images/image-006.jpg)

If you see **You have free Azure Advisor recommendations!**, close the window to continue the lab.

If a **Welcome to Microsoft Azure** pop-up window appears, click **Maybe Later** to skip the tour.  
![](images/image-007.jpg)

Click **Next** from the bottom right corner to embark on your lab journey.  
![](images/s36.png)

Now you’re all set to explore. If you have any questions along the way, feel free to reach out. Enjoy your workshop!
