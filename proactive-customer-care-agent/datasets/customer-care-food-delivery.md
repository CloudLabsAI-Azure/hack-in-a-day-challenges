## 1. Ordering Process

### 1.1 Placing an Order

**How to Order Food:**
1. Open the app or visit website
2. Enter delivery address
3. Browse restaurants in your area
4. Select items from menu
5. Add special instructions if needed
6. Review cart and apply promo codes
7. Choose payment method
8. Place order

**Order Confirmation:**
- Immediate confirmation displayed in app
- Email confirmation sent within 1 minute
- SMS updates for order status
- Estimated delivery time provided

**Order Customization:**
- Add special instructions for each item
- Request extra condiments or utensils
- Specify dietary restrictions
- Note preferences for contactless delivery

### 1.2 Modifying Orders

**Order Changes:**
- Modifications possible within 2 minutes of placement
- Contact restaurant directly for changes after 2 minutes
- Some restaurants may not accept modifications once order confirmed
- Cancellation possible before restaurant starts preparing

**Adding Items:**
- Cannot add items to existing order
- Place new separate order if needed
- Both orders may arrive at different times
- Delivery fees apply to each order separately

### 1.3 Order Cancellation

**Cancellation Policy:**
- Free cancellation within 2 minutes of order placement
- After 2 minutes, cancellation fee may apply
- No cancellation once food preparation starts
- Full refund if order cancelled by restaurant

**How to Cancel:**
1. Go to "Active Orders" in app
2. Select order to cancel
3. Tap "Cancel Order"
4. Select cancellation reason
5. Confirm cancellation

**Cancellation Fees:**
- 0-2 minutes: No fee
- 2-5 minutes: $3 cancellation fee
- 5+ minutes or food prep started: Non-refundable

## 2. Delivery Information

### 2.1 Delivery Options

**Standard Delivery:**
- Estimated time: 30-45 minutes
- Delivery fee: $2.99-$5.99 based on distance
- Real-time tracking available
- Leave-at-door option available

**Priority Delivery:**
- Estimated time: 20-30 minutes
- Additional $3.99 priority fee
- Dedicated driver assignment
- Faster restaurant preparation

**Scheduled Delivery:**
- Order up to 4 days in advance
- Schedule specific delivery time
- Minimum 2 hours advance notice
- No additional fees

### 2.2 Delivery Tracking

**Track Your Order:**
- Real-time map tracking in app
- Status updates via push notifications
- SMS updates available
- Driver contact information provided

**Order Status Stages:**
- **Restaurant Received:** Restaurant confirmed order
- **Preparing:** Food is being prepared
- **Ready for Pickup:** Driver assigned, heading to restaurant
- **Picked Up:** Driver has collected your order
- **Arriving Soon:** Driver is 5 minutes away
- **Delivered:** Order completed

**Driver Information:**
- Driver name and photo
- Vehicle information
- Call or text driver directly through app
- Driver location on map

### 2.3 Delivery Issues

**Late Delivery:**
- Check app for real-time updates
- Traffic and weather may cause delays
- Contact support if delivery exceeds estimate by 20 minutes
- Refund or credit issued for significant delays

**Wrong Address:**
- Verify delivery address before ordering
- Update address in app settings
- Cannot redirect order once driver dispatched
- Additional delivery fee for address corrections

**Missing Items:**
- Check order contents immediately upon delivery
- Report missing items within 2 hours
- Full refund or redelivery for missing items
- Photograph order contents if possible

**Quality Issues:**
- Report quality problems within 1 hour of delivery
- Include photos of food quality issues
- Refund or credit issued for legitimate complaints
- Restaurant notified of quality concerns

## 3. Food Quality and Safety

### 3.1 Food Safety Standards

**Restaurant Verification:**
- All restaurants verified with health department licenses
- Regular compliance checks
- Food safety ratings displayed
- Customer reviews and ratings available

**Tamper-Evident Packaging:**
- Sealed bags and containers required
- Do not accept delivery if packaging appears tampered
- Report packaging concerns immediately
- Reorder at no cost if packaging compromised

**Temperature Requirements:**
- Hot food maintained above 140 degrees F
- Cold food kept below 40 degrees F
- Insulated delivery bags used
- Contactless thermometer checks available

### 3.2 Dietary Information

**Allergen Information:**
- Menu items labeled with common allergens
- Contact restaurant for detailed allergen info
- Special dietary filters available (gluten-free, vegan, etc.)
- Not liable for undisclosed allergens

**Nutritional Information:**
- Calorie counts displayed when available
- Full nutritional details on restaurant websites
- Healthy option filters in app
- Restaurant responsible for accuracy

**Special Diets:**
- Filter by dietary preference (vegetarian, vegan, keto, etc.)
- Add notes about restrictions
- Verify with restaurant for cross-contamination concerns
- Specialty diet restaurants available

### 3.3 Food Quality Complaints

**Quality Standards:**
- Food should be hot, fresh, and properly packaged
- Incorrect items or wrong orders eligible for refund
- Temperature issues result in credit or redelivery
- Poor quality consistently addressed with restaurant

**How to Report:**
1. Go to order history
2. Select problematic order
3. Tap "Get Help"
4. Choose issue type
5. Upload photos if applicable
6. Submit complaint

**Resolution Options:**
- Full or partial refund to original payment method
- Account credit for future orders
- Redelivery of correct items
- Restaurant rating and feedback

## 4. Payment and Billing

### 4.1 Payment Methods

**Accepted Payments:**
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Gift cards and promotional credits
- Cash on delivery (select areas only)

**Payment Security:**
- Encrypted payment processing
- PCI compliant systems
- Card details never shared with restaurants or drivers
- Secure authentication required

**Saved Payment Methods:**
- Store multiple payment methods
- Set default payment option
- Update or remove cards anytime
- Expired card notifications

### 4.2 Pricing and Fees

**Order Total Breakdown:**
- Subtotal: Cost of food items
- Delivery Fee: $2.99-$5.99 based on distance
- Service Fee: 10-15% of subtotal
- Taxes: Based on local tax rates
- Tip: Recommended 15-20% (customizable)

**Fee Waivers:**
- Free delivery with subscription membership
- Reduced service fees for members
- Minimum order requirements for free delivery
- Restaurant-specific promotions

### 4.3 Billing Issues

**Incorrect Charges:**
- Review itemized receipt in order history
- Verify all items and modifications
- Check for applied discounts and promotions
- Contact support within 48 hours

**Duplicate Charges:**
- Holds may appear as duplicate charges
- Actual charges processed within 3-5 business days
- Refunds for genuine duplicates within 5-7 business days
- Bank authorization holds released automatically

**Refunds:**
- Processed to original payment method
- Appear in account within 5-7 business days
- Account credit instant for future orders
- Refund policy based on issue type

## 8. Technical Support

### 8.1 App Issues

**Common App Problems:**
- Force close and reopen app
- Check for app updates
- Clear app cache
- Reinstall app if issues persist
- Check device storage space

**Login Issues:**
- Verify email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Check for account suspension
- Contact support for account access

### 8.2 Website Issues

**Browser Problems:**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try incognito mode
- Switch to different browser
- Disable browser extensions
- Update browser version

**Checkout Errors:**
- Verify payment information
- Check internet connection
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact support with error message

### 8.3 GPS and Location

**Location Services:**
- Enable location access in device settings
- Grant app location permissions
- Use manual address entry if GPS inaccurate
- Report location issues to support

**Delivery Tracking:**
- Refresh map for latest updates
- Check driver's ETA
- Contact driver if off route
- Report tracking issues
