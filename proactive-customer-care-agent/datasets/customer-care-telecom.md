**Online Account Portal:**

**Mobile App Features:**
- Account management on the go

### 1.2 Account Security

**Privacy Settings:**

### 1.3 Making Changes to Account

**Removing Lines:**

- Number port out available

**Transferring Service:**
- Transfer number between accounts

- No service interruption

## 2. Billing and Payments

### 2.1 Understanding Your Bill

**Bill Components:**
- Monthly service charges
- Device payment installments
- Taxes and regulatory fees
- Usage charges (if applicable)
- One-time charges and credits

**Billing Cycle:**
- Monthly billing on same date each month
- Bill available 5-7 days before due date
- View current and past bills online
- Paperless billing available
- Detailed usage breakdowns included

**First Bill:**
- May be higher than typical monthly bill
- Includes activation fees
- Pro-rated charges for partial month
- Advance billing for next cycle
- Device down payment if applicable

### 2.2 Payment Options

**Payment Methods:**
- Autopay (credit/debit card or bank account)
- Online payment via account portal
- Mobile app payment
- Phone payment (automated or with agent)
- In-store payment
- Mail payment (allow 5-7 days)

**Autopay Benefits:**
- $10 monthly discount per line
- Never miss a payment
- Automatic on due date
- Update payment method anytime
- Email notification before payment

**Payment Arrangements:**
- Extension available once per year
- Up to 7-day extension
- Must call before due date
- Small convenience fee may apply
- Prevents service interruption

### 2.3 Billing Issues

**Disputed Charges:**
- Review detailed bill online
- Contact customer service within 60 days
- Provide account number and details
- Investigation within 1-2 billing cycles
- Credits applied if error confirmed

**Late Payments:**
- $5 late fee after grace period
- Service interruption possible after 30 days
- Restore service after payment received
- $20 reconnection fee may apply
- Payment plans available for large balances

**Overpayment Refunds:**
- Applied as credit to next bill
- Refund check available upon request
- Process takes 2-3 billing cycles
- Refunds for closed accounts automatic
- No refund for amounts under $5

## 5. Network and Coverage

### 5.1 Coverage Information

**Network Coverage:**
- 99% population coverage nationwide
- 5G available in major cities
- 4G LTE everywhere else
- Coverage map available online
- Roaming agreements for additional coverage

**Coverage Issues:**
- Report dead zones
- Check for network outages
- Wi-Fi calling alternative
- Signal booster options available
- Coverage guarantee in some areas

**Roaming:**
- Domestic roaming included
- International roaming additional
- Data roaming can be disabled
- Roaming charges vary by location
- Travel passes recommended for international

### 5.2 Network Performance

**5G Service:**
- Fastest speeds available
- 5G-compatible device required
- Available in expanding areas
- Falls back to 4G when unavailable
- No additional charge for 5G access

**Data Speeds:**
- 5G speeds up to 1 Gbps
- 4G LTE speeds 5-100 Mbps
- Network management during congestion
- Video optimization on some plans
- Speed tests available in app

**Network Congestion:**
- May experience slower speeds during peak times
- Priority data on premium plans
- Temporary slowdowns in crowded areas
- Normal speeds resume after congestion
- Wi-Fi alternative recommended

### 5.3 Wi-Fi Services

**Wi-Fi Calling:**
- Make calls over Wi-Fi connection
- No additional charge
- Same quality as cellular
- Enable in device settings
- Works internationally

**Wi-Fi Hotspots:**
- Free at company locations
- Automatic connection
- Secure encrypted network
- Millions of hotspots nationwide
- Save cellular data
