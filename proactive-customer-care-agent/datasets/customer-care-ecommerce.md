## 1. Order Management

### 1.1 Order Placement

**How to Place an Order:**
1. Browse products and add items to your shopping cart
2. Click "Proceed to Checkout" when ready
3. Enter or confirm shipping address
4. Select shipping method
5. Choose payment method
6. Review order summary
7. Click "Place Order" to complete purchase

**Order Confirmation:**
- You will receive an order confirmation email within 5 minutes
- Email includes order number, items purchased, and estimated delivery date
- Save this email for tracking and reference

**Modifying Orders:**
- Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement
- Contact customer service immediately if changes are needed
- After 1 hour, orders enter processing and cannot be modified
- Cancellation may be possible before shipping

### 1.2 Order Tracking

**Tracking Your Order:**
1. Visit the Order Tracking page on our website
2. Enter your order number and email address
3. Click "Track Order" to view current status

**Order Status Definitions:**
- **Order Received:** Your order has been received and is being processed
- **Processing:** Items are being picked and packed in our warehouse
- **Shipped:** Your order has been dispatched and is in transit
- **Out for Delivery:** Package is with delivery driver for final delivery
- **Delivered:** Package has been successfully delivered

**Tracking Number:**
- Tracking numbers are emailed once your order ships
- Usually within 24-48 hours of order placement
- Use tracking number on carrier website for detailed updates

### 1.3 Order Cancellation

**Cancellation Policy:**
- Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement
- Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled but can be returned
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days after cancellation

**How to Cancel:**
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select order to cancel
4. Click "Cancel Order" button
5. Confirm cancellation

## 2. Shipping and Delivery

### 2.1 Shipping Options

**Standard Shipping:**
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $5.99 or FREE on orders over $50
- Available nationwide

**Express Shipping:**
- Delivery time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99
- Available in major cities

**Next Day Delivery:**
- Delivery time: 1 business day
- Cost: $24.99
- Order before 2 PM for next day delivery
- Available in select metropolitan areas

### 2.2 Delivery Areas

**Domestic Shipping:**
- Available to all 50 states
- PO Box addresses accepted for standard shipping
- APO/FPO addresses accepted with extended delivery times

**International Shipping:**
- Available to select countries
- Additional customs fees may apply
- Delivery times vary by destination (10-21 business days)

### 2.3 Delivery Issues

**Missing Package:**
- Check with neighbors or building reception
- Verify delivery address in order confirmation
- Contact carrier using tracking number
- File claim if package marked delivered but not received
- We will reship or refund after carrier investigation

**Damaged Package:**
- Inspect package before accepting delivery
- Photograph damaged packaging and contents
- Contact customer service within 48 hours
- Return damaged items for replacement or refund

**Delayed Delivery:**
- Check tracking information for updates
- Weather and carrier delays may occur
- Contact customer service if delivery exceeds estimated date by 3 days
- We will investigate and provide resolution

## 3. Returns and Refunds

### 3.1 Return Policy

**General Return Policy:**
- 30-day return window from delivery date
- Items must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging
- Original tags and labels must be attached
- Proof of purchase required

**Non-Returnable Items:**
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Intimate apparel and swimwear
- Perishable goods
- Digital downloads and gift cards
- Final sale items

### 3.2 Return Process

**How to Initiate Return:**
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select order with item to return
4. Click "Return Item"
5. Select reason for return
6. Print return shipping label

**Return Shipping:**
- FREE return shipping for defective or incorrect items
- Customer pays return shipping for buyer's remorse
- Use provided prepaid label or ship via preferred carrier
- Keep tracking number for proof of return

**Return Address:**
```
Returns Department
Contoso E-Commerce
1234 Warehouse Drive
Distribution Center, ST 12345
```

### 3.3 Refund Process

**Refund Timeline:**
- Refund processed within 3-5 business days of receiving return
- Original payment method will be credited
- Allow 5-7 business days for refund to appear in account

**Refund Amount:**
- Full purchase price refunded for defective items
- Original shipping charges refunded if item defective
- Partial refund for damaged but usable items (with customer approval)

**Store Credit Option:**
- Choose store credit for instant processing
- 10% bonus added to store credit amount
- No expiration date on store credit

## 5. Account Management

### 5.1 Creating an Account

**Account Benefits:**
- Faster checkout process
- Order history and tracking
- Saved addresses and payment methods
- Exclusive member promotions
- Wishlist and favorites

**How to Create Account:**
1. Click "Sign Up" on homepage
2. Enter email address and create password
3. Complete profile information
4. Verify email address via confirmation link
5. Start shopping

### 5.2 Account Security

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
- Change password every 90 days for security

**Account Protection:**
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Never share login credentials
- Review account activity regularly
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### 5.3 Profile Management

**Updating Information:**
- Edit profile in "My Account" section
- Update email, phone number, and addresses
- Manage payment methods
- Set communication preferences

**Account Deletion:**
- Contact customer service to delete account
- All order history will be archived
- Personal information removed per privacy policy
- Process takes 5-7 business days

## 6. Payment and Billing

### 6.1 Payment Methods

**Accepted Payment Methods:**
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards with credit card logos
- PayPal and digital wallets
- Store gift cards and credit
- Buy Now Pay Later options (Affirm, Klarna)

**Payment Security:**
- SSL encryption for all transactions
- PCI-DSS compliant processing
- Card information never stored on our servers
- 3D Secure authentication for added protection

### 6.2 Billing Issues

**Payment Declined:**
- Verify card information is correct
- Check with bank for transaction limits
- Ensure sufficient funds available
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact bank if issues persist

**Incorrect Charge:**
- Review order confirmation and receipt
- Check for pre-authorization holds (released in 3-5 days)
- Contact customer service with order number
- Dispute resolution within 10 business days

### 6.3 Invoices and Receipts

**Invoice Access:**
- Digital invoice emailed with order confirmation
- Download invoice from "My Orders" section
- Request printed invoice via customer service

**Tax Information:**
- Sales tax calculated based on shipping address
- Tax rates vary by state and locality
- Tax-exempt purchases require documentation
- Contact customer service for tax questions

## 8. Technical Support

### 8.1 Website Issues

**Common Website Problems:**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Disable browser extensions
- Check internet connection
- Update browser to latest version

**Mobile App Support:**
- Update app to latest version
- Clear app cache in device settings
- Uninstall and reinstall if issues persist
- Check device storage space
- Ensure operating system is up to date

### 8.2 Checkout Problems

**Can't Complete Checkout:**
- Verify all required fields completed
- Check payment information accuracy
- Ensure items in cart are in stock
- Try guest checkout option
- Contact customer service for assistance

**Error Messages:**
- Screenshot error message
- Note order number if provided
- Clear browser cache and retry
- Contact technical support with details

### 8.3 Email Communication

**Not Receiving Emails:**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Add our email to safe sender list
- Verify email address in account settings
- Check email provider's blocked list
- Allow 24 hours for system updates
