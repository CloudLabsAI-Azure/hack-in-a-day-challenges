## 4. Returns and Exchanges

### 4.1 Return Policy

**General Policy:**
- 60-day return window
- Original receipt required
- Items in original condition with tags
- Packaging and accessories must be included
- Proof of purchase required

**Extended Holiday Returns:**
- October - December purchases returnable until January 31
- Gift receipts allow recipient to return
- Original payment method refund or store credit

**Exceptions:**
- Final sale items non-returnable
- Opened cosmetics and personal care (hygiene)
- Custom or personalized items
- Gift cards

### 4.2 Return Process

**In-Store Returns:**
1. Bring item and original receipt to any store location
2. Visit customer service desk
3. Present identification for non-receipted returns
4. Receive refund or exchange
5. Alterations department for tailored items

**Online Returns:**
1. Initiate return in order history
2. Print prepaid return label
3. Package item securely
4. Drop off at carrier location or schedule pickup
5. Track return shipment
6. Refund processed upon receipt

**Return Shipping:**
- Free return shipping on all online orders
- Use provided prepaid label
- Original shipping charges not refunded
- Exceptions for defective or incorrect items

### 4.3 Exchanges

**Exchange Policy:**
- Free exchanges for different size or color
- Exchange for different item at price difference
- In-store or online exchanges accepted
- Original item must be returned first for online exchanges

**Even Exchange Process:**
1. Return original item
2. New item shipped immediately or given in store
3. No additional charges for same-price exchanges
4. Price adjustments for higher or lower priced items

## 7. Payment and Financing

### 7.1 Payment Options

**Accepted Payments:**
- All major credit and debit cards
- Store credit card (special financing)
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay)
- Gift cards and store credit
- Cash (in-store only)
- Buy Now Pay Later partners (Affirm, Klarna)

**Store Credit Card:**
- Special financing on purchases over $199
- 5% back on all purchases
- Exclusive cardholder events
- No annual fee
- Apply in-store or online

### 7.2 Price Adjustments

**Price Match Policy:**
- Match competitor pricing on identical items
- Bring proof of competitor price
- Must be in stock at competitor
- Price match within 7 days of purchase
- Excludes closeout and clearance items

**Sale Price Adjustments:**
- Item goes on sale within 14 days of purchase
- Bring receipt for price adjustment
- Difference refunded to original payment method
- One adjustment per item

### 7.3 Financing Options

**Special Financing:**
- 0% APR for 6-12 months on qualifying purchases
- $199 minimum purchase
- Store credit card required
- Deferred interest promotion
- Pay in full before promo ends to avoid interest

**Layaway Program:**
- Available September - December
- 20% down payment required
- 8-week payment plan
- $10 service fee
- Cancel anytime for refund minus service fee
